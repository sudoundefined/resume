# LinkedIn Profile Optimization Guide

LinkedIn parses keywords very similarly to an ATS system, but the format should be slightly more conversational (especially in the "About" section). Here is exactly what to copy and paste into your LinkedIn profile to maximize your visibility to recruiters hiring for Technical Lead and Senior Node/React roles.

---

## 1. The Headline
*Your headline is the #1 most important factor for LinkedIn SEO. Choose one of these options that best fits your style:*

**Option 1 (Highly Targeted - Recommended):**
> Technical Lead | Senior Full-Stack Engineer | Node.js, React.js, Angular, TypeScript | Architecting Scalable Distributed Systems

**Option 2 (Impact & Leadership Focused):**
> Senior Software Engineer & Technical Lead | Node.js & React.js Expert | Cloud Architecture & Microservices | Driving CI/CD Automation

---

## 2. The "About" Section
*LinkedIn summaries perform best when written in the first person. This is an optimized adaptation of your resume's Professional Summary.*

> I am an accomplished Technical Lead and Senior Full-Stack Engineer with over 9 years of experience architecting highly scalable, service-oriented distributed systems. My technical expertise spans JavaScript, Node.js, TypeScript, React.js, and Angular, allowing me to build end-to-end enterprise web applications that drive real business value.

Most recently, my work has delivered up to 40% in operational cost savings through architecture modernizations and CI/CD automation.

> As a highly collaborative leader, I take full responsibility for my team's Scaling, Performance, & Quality. I am deeply passionate about driving technology choices, defining project scope alongside stakeholders, establishing robust engineering best practices, and troubleshooting complex technical issues to deliver highly available, secure solutions.

> 🚀 Core Competencies: Node.js, React.js, Angular, TypeScript, Microservices (SOA), Event-Driven Architectures, Multi-Tenancy, Single-SPA, CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes.
